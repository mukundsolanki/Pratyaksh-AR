About Us

We are team SyntaxSorcerers from Indore and here at HackCBS 6.0 we developed a Smart Companion Argumental Glasses for people Suffering from Hearing Impaired. Making people independent on their own with the help of emerging cutting edge technology at a effective cost.

Realtime Low Latency Transcription

Our AR Smart glasses serve as a beacon of innovation, redefining real-time communication for individuals with hearing impairments. We have harnessed the power of Augmented Reality (AR) to create a seamless, intuitive, and life-changing solution. By leveraging advanced speech recognition algorithms and augmented reality technologies, our glasses transcribe conversations in real-time, converting spoken words into visible text.

Multi Language Pair Support

In our quest for inclusivity, we have expanded the horizons of our AR Smart glasses to support multiple languages. Understanding that communication is diverse and dynamic, we have incorporated advanced multilingual support. Our glasses can seamlessly transcribe conversations from a variety of languages, making interactions effortless for users regardless of the language they speak.

Industry Leading WER of 2%

we have achieved an outstanding accuracy rate of 94.6% in transcribing conversations. This remarkable precision ensures that every spoken word is captured faithfully, empowering users with a reliable tool for seamless communication. Our advanced algorithms, coupled with machine learning techniques, have been fine-tuned to guarantee unparalleled accuracy in diverse linguistic contexts.

Effective Cost

Our affordability approach is rooted in empathy. We recognize the financial challenges faced by individuals with hearing impairments and the organizations that support them. By making our AR Smart glasses affordable, we are ensuring that this transformative technology reaches those who need it the most. Our mission is to empower, and affordability is a key component of our empowerment strategy.

Seamless User Experience

Our cost-effectiveness does not compromise the quality or functionality of our AR Smart glasses. We maintain the highest standards of performance, accuracy, and user experience while keeping the costs low. Users can enjoy the benefits of cutting-edge technology without the burden of hefty price tags, making our solution the ideal choice for individuals, families, and organizations seeking a reliable and budget-friendly communication tool.
